OLLC HISTORY

The our Lady of Lourdes College was initially concieve by its founder Mr. Alfredo D. Demetillo and Dr. Adelia C. Demetillo as and educational institution primarily to serve the educational needs of the youth of valenzuela City. The college is located at Gen. T. De Leon, Valenzuela City. The Pre-School was officially established in 1986 bearing the name Cherub Learning Center with modest enrolment of 17 kindergarten students. The grade school opened in 1987 with 222 students from nursery to Grade Three. Twenty students became the first graduates of the elementary level. Four years later, 88 students of the first batch graduated from the high school department. In just a few years this learning institution has grown far beyond what was originally envisioned. Armed with sheer courage but with an unfailing faith in the divine providence, Mr. And Mrs. Alfredo Demetillo formally opened the College department in 1994. Like the basic education department, the College also had a humble beginning. Four courses were offered, namely Bachelor of Science in Secondary Education, Bachelor of Science in Elementary Education, Business Administration and Bachelor of Science in Accountancy. Buoyed, by the growing population, Mr. And Mrs. Alfredo Demetillo decided to construct the administration building along the Gen. T. De Leon St. This major construction houses several classrooms, an auditorium, a spacious library, a complete laboratory, HE room, Audio Visual room, HRM Housekeeping rooms, mini hotel and front service amenities, college office and several administrative offices that includes the president's office, Human Resource and Management Office, principal's offices and a registrar accounting office Various student services units that serve the needs of the students were systematized. They include the Medical and Dental Clinic, Guidance Library Science Business Laboratories, Computer Lab., HELE Practice House, Workshop. Bus Service and Canteen. The School is accessible to all types of transportations. The institutional administration was organized under the leadership of Mr. Alfredo Demetillo as president of the Board of Trustees. He is ably assisted by the Directress Dr. Adelia Demetillo, and four Vice Presidents; VP for Academic Affairs, VP for Finance, VP for Planning and Development and VP for External Affairs. The growth of OLLC is accelerative in both the human and material resources providing the academic community with an environment conducive to learning. The colourful and dynamic history of the Our Lady of Lourdes College signifies a commitment to a continuing development in the pursuit of quality education that its clientele deserve.
